What is This?

This is a tool for travelers to plan their journey. You can search for exciting places around your desired location. There will be description, pictures, and reviews for you to decide whether you want to go to the place or not. If you are interested in a place, you can add it to your plan. You can specify which days and hours to go to the place. Happy Traveling!
